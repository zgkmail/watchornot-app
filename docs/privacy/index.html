Privacy Policy for

Watch or Skip

Last Updated: November 26, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Watch or Skip is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Watch or Skip, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: User ID and authentication tokens for account management
  • Movie Ratings: Your votes (Like, Dislike, Skip) on movies and TV shows
  • Viewing History: Records of movies you've rated to provide personalized recommendations
  • Camera Photos: Photos you take with the camera to identify movies (processed temporarily and deleted immediately after analysis)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Camera Access: Temporary access to camera for movie poster identification (photos are not stored)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We integrate with the following third-party services to provide our features:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database): Movie metadata, posters, and information
  • OMDb API: IMDb ratings, Rotten Tomatoes, and Metacritic scores
  • Claude AI (Anthropic): Image analysis for movie identification from photos
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services (if using free version)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Identify movies from photos you take with your camera
  • Store your movie ratings and voting history
  • Generate personalized recommendations based on your taste profile
  • Display movie information, ratings, and metadata

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Optimize app performance and user experience

2.3 Advertising (Free Version Only)

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Measure ad performance and engagement
  • Respect your ad tracking preferences set in iOS Settings

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Backend Server: Hosted on Fly.io (secure cloud infrastructure)
  • Local Device: Authentication tokens stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • Image Processing: Photos are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after analysis

3.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication using UUID-based user identification
  • iOS Keychain storage for sensitive credentials
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No storage of credit card information (payments handled by Apple)

3.3 Data Retention

  • Ratings and History: Stored indefinitely until you delete them or your account
  • Camera Photos: Deleted immediately after movie identification
  • Cache Data: Cleared automatically after expiration (24 hours to 90 days depending on data type)

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers We Use

We share minimal necessary data with the following service providers:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Camera photos you take for movie identification (not stored by Anthropic)
  • TMDB: Movie search queries (no personal information)
  • OMDb: IMDb IDs to fetch ratings (no personal information)
  • Google AdMob: Device identifiers for ad serving (respects App Tracking Transparency)
  • Fly.io: Hosting infrastructure for backend services

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • View Your Data: Access your rating history anytime in the History tab
  • Delete Ratings: Remove individual movie ratings from your history
  • Delete Account: Contact us to request complete account deletion
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data (contact us)

5.2 App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

We respect Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework:

  • You can control ad tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • We request permission before tracking across other apps or websites
  • You can opt out of personalized ads at any time

5.3 Camera Access

  • You control camera permissions in iOS Settings
  • You can revoke camera access at any time (movie identification feature will be unavailable)
  • Photos taken are processed immediately and never stored

6. Children's Privacy

Watch or Skip is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

7. International Users

Our services are hosted in the United States. If you are accessing the app from outside the US, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

8. In-App Purchases

8.1 Remove Ads Purchase

  • Processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Purchase records managed by Apple (subject to Apple's privacy policy)
  • We only receive confirmation of purchase completion

9. Advertising

9.1 Google AdMob (Free Version)

  • Displays banner and interstitial ads
  • May collect device identifiers and usage data
  • Subject to Google's privacy policy
  • Respects your iOS ad tracking preferences
  • Can be removed with "Remove Ads" in-app purchase

9.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Purchase "Remove Ads" to disable all advertising
  • Disable ad personalization in iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Use iOS "Limit Ad Tracking" feature

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
